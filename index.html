WHAT IS A SOFT SKILL

As the name implies(soft skills)SOFT skills are non-technical skills that enhance an individual's ability to interact,communicate,and work effectively with others.They encompass personal attributes,personality traits,and social skills that complement hard skills(technical expertise).

Examples of soft skills

  1. Communication
  2. teamwork
  3. Time management
  4. Problem solving
  5. Adaptability
  6. Creativity

There are many more examples of soft skills apart from this ones listed so explore more......

importance Of Soft Skills

  1. Enhance career prospects
  2. Improves relationship with colleagues and clients
  3. Increase productivity and efficiency
  4. Boost job satisfaction and engagement
  5. Foster a positive work culture

As you can see this are some of the importance of soft skills

Benefits/Conclusion on soft skills

Having a good soft skills helps one in many areas in life,in the fact that it enhances your career prospect and it also paves way for you.
